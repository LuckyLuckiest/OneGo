Places to visit


Tokyo is a vibrant and bustling city located in the heart of Japan. As the capital of Japan, Tokyo is one of the most populous cities in the world, with a population of over 14 million people. It is known for its vibrant culture, cutting-edge technology, and rich history. Tokyo is also home to a diverse range of neighborhoods, each with its own unique character and attractions. Visitors to Tokyo can enjoy world-class dining, shopping, and entertainment, as well as explore the city's many temples, museums, and parks.


Istanbul is a city that straddles two continents, Asia and Europe, making it a unique and fascinating destination. It is the largest city in Turkey, with a population of over 15 million people, and has a rich history that spans over two millennia. Istanbul has been the capital of three empires - the Roman, Byzantine, and Ottoman - and is known for its stunning architecture, including the Hagia Sophia and the Blue Mosque. Visitors can also explore the city's bustling bazaars and sample delicious Turkish cuisine.


New York, also known as the "Big Apple," is one of the most iconic cities in the world. It is located in the northeastern United States and is home to over 8 million people, making it the most populous city in the country. New York is known for its towering skyscrapers, such as the Empire State Building and the One World Trade Center, as well as its famous landmarks like the Statue of Liberty and Central Park. Visitors to New York can enjoy world-class dining, shopping, and entertainment.
